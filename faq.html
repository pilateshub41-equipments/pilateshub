Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our equipment, delivery, and after-sales support. Can't find your answer? Just reach out.

Buying & Equipment
Are your equipment suitable for home use?
Yes! We have equipment designed for both home and studio use. Our team can help you choose the right equipment based on your space and fitness goals. We offer compact models ideal for home settings, as well as professional-grade machines for commercial studios.
What types of Pilates equipment do you sell?
We supply a comprehensive range of professional Pilates equipment including Reformers, Cadillacs (Trapeze Tables), Stability Chairs, Ladder Barrels, and Swedish Ladders. We also carry accessories such as Pilates Sticks, Wooden Wobble Boards, and Exercise Mats — everything needed to fully equip a studio or home practice space.
Can beginners use Pilates equipment at home?
Absolutely. Most of our equipment — particularly the Reformer and Stability Chair — is beginner-friendly with adjustable spring resistance and clear setup guides. We recommend starting with a few guided sessions (in-studio or online) before solo home use, to get comfortable with the machine and proper form.
Delivery & Installation
Do you ship equipment across India?
Yes, we deliver pan-India — from metro cities to Tier-2 and Tier-3 towns. All equipment is carefully packed and shipped via trusted logistics partners. Delivery timelines vary by location, typically 7–15 business days. We'll keep you updated at every stage of your shipment.
Do you offer assembly or installation support?
Yes. For major equipment like Reformers, Cadillacs, and Swedish Ladders, we provide professional assembly and installation support. Our technicians will set up the equipment at your location, verify all springs and components, and walk you through proper use. This service is available in most cities — contact us to confirm availability in your area.
What if my equipment is damaged during delivery?
If your equipment arrives damaged, please photograph the damage and contact us within 48 hours of delivery via WhatsApp or email. We will arrange a replacement or repair at no cost to you. All our shipments are insured, and we take full responsibility for transit damage.
Warranty & Service
What is the warranty on your equipment?
All major equipment comes with a manufacturer warranty — typically 1 year on the frame and mechanical parts, and 6 months on upholstery and springs. Warranty terms may vary by product and brand. Please ask our team for the specific warranty details for the equipment you are interested in before placing an order.
How do I maintain and clean my Pilates equipment?
Wipe down upholstered surfaces after each use with a mild disinfectant spray or damp cloth. Metal frames can be wiped with a dry cloth — avoid harsh chemicals. Springs should be inspected periodically for wear and replaced as needed. We provide a care guide with every purchase, and our after-sales team is always available if you have questions.
Can I try the equipment before purchasing?
We understand that Pilates equipment is a significant investment. Where possible, we can arrange a demonstration at a partner studio near you. Contact us with your city and the equipment you are interested in, and we will do our best to connect you with a trial opportunity before you commit to a purchase.

Still have questions?

Our team is available Mon–Sat, 9am–6pm. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
