Pilates Reformer

The Reformer is the flagship of Pilates training — a spring-resistance apparatus that delivers a full-body workout for strength, flexibility, coordination and control. Its unique system of springs, pulleys and a sliding carriage allows for hundreds of exercises suited to all levels, from rehabilitation to elite athletic training.

Key Features

Vertical Support Frame with Spring Connectors
Multi-level Targeted Resistance Springs
Cushioned Platform & Shoulder Support Blocks
Adjustable HeadRest
Adjustable Footbar & Foot Support Loop
Rope Pulley Mechanism
Padded Jump Platform (add-on)
Caster Wheels for repositioning
Padded Support Box included
